 ● Invace Spaders


+ GAME INFO    + SHARE

Invace Spaders is a simple space adventure game, where you try to defeat as many aliens as possible before being destroyed. This is and endless game, where the player can go on forever as long as it’s alive. There are two bonuses in the game: One slowing down enemies for 5 seconds when it’s been collected by the player, and the other spawning a “laser line” which clears out every enemy on the screen.
